Recipe #1

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Chicken breasts 4
Olive oil 2 tablespoons
Garlic cloves, minced 3
  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper.
  3. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium-high heat.
  4. Add minced garlic and sauté until fragrant.
  5. Add chicken breasts and cook until browned on both sides.
  6. Transfer the chicken to a baking dish and bake for 20 minutes.

Recipe #2

  • Time: 45 minutes
  • Serves: 6
Ingredients Quantity
Pasta 1 pound
Tomatoes 4
Onion 1
  1. Cook pasta according to package instructions.
  2. Dice tomatoes and onions.
  3. Sauté onions in olive oil until translucent.
  4. Add tomatoes and cook until they break down.
  5. Toss cooked pasta with the tomato sauce.

Recipe #3

  • Time: 60 minutes
  • Serves: 8
Ingredients Quantity
Beef stew meat 2 pounds
Carrots, chopped 4
Potatoes, diced 4
  1. Brown the beef stew meat in a large pot.
  2. Add chopped carrots, diced potatoes, and enough water to cover the ingredients.
  3. Simmer for 45 minutes until the meat is tender.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
